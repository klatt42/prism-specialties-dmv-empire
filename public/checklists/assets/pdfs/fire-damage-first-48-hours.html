IMMEDIATE SAFETY (0-30 MINUTES)
🚨 Ensure Everyone is Safe
Evacuate all persons and pets. Do not re-enter until fire officials declare it safe.
📞 Contact Emergency Services
Call 911 if not already done. Report gas leaks, electrical hazards, or structural damage.
🏠 Check Structural Safety
Look for sagging ceilings, damaged walls, or compromised support beams.
EMERGENCY RESPONSE (30 MIN - 2 HOURS)
📱 Contact Insurance Company
Report claim immediately. Get claim number and adjuster contact information.
📸 Document Everything
Take extensive photos/videos of all damage before moving anything.
🔒 Secure the Property
Board up broken windows/openings to prevent further damage.
DAMAGE CONTROL (2-8 HOURS)
💧 Address Water Damage
Remove standing water from firefighting efforts if electricity is safe.
🌬️ Ventilate Smoke Odors
Open windows/doors carefully. Use fans without spreading soot.
📋 Create Damage Inventory
List all damaged items with descriptions and estimated values.
