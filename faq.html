How quickly can you respond to emergencies?

We guarantee response within 90 minutes anywhere in the DMV region, with most emergency calls receiving attention within 60 minutes. Our emergency response includes immediate damage assessment, stabilization procedures, and transport to our secure facility.

What should I do immediately after damage occurs?

Emergency steps:

  1. Ensure Safety: Make sure the area is safe to enter
  2. Contact Us Immediately: Call our 24/7 emergency line
  3. Document Damage: Take photos for insurance if safe to do so
  4. Don't Attempt Cleaning: Improper cleaning can cause permanent damage
  5. Control Environment: Maintain stable temperature and humidity if possible
  6. Contact Insurance: Notify your insurance company of the claim

Do you provide emergency services during holidays and weekends?

Yes, our emergency response operates 365 days a year, including all holidays and weekends. Damage doesn't wait for business hours, and neither do we.

Art Restoration

What types of artwork do you restore?

We restore all types of fine art including:

  • Oil and acrylic paintings
  • Watercolors and works on paper
  • Sculptures and three-dimensional art
  • Antique and contemporary frames
  • Mixed media and installation pieces
  • Historic and contemporary works

How long does art restoration typically take?

Timeline varies by project complexity:

  • Minor cleaning/repair: 1-2 weeks
  • Standard restoration: 3-6 weeks
  • Complex restoration: 2-4 months
  • Emergency situations: Rush service available

We provide detailed timelines with each project estimate.

Can you restore water-damaged or fire-damaged artwork?

Yes, we specialize in emergency art restoration including water damage, fire damage, and smoke damage. Quick response is critical - contact us immediately after damage occurs. Many pieces that appear completely damaged can be successfully restored with proper emergency treatment.

Do you work on family heirlooms and sentimental pieces?

Absolutely. We understand the emotional value of family artwork and treat every piece with the same care and attention, regardless of market value. Many of our most rewarding projects involve restoring meaningful family pieces for future generations.

Electronics Restoration

What types of electronics do you restore?

Our electronics restoration services include:

  • Vintage audio equipment and stereo systems
  • Church and institutional sound systems
  • Musical instrument electronics
  • Computer and office equipment
  • Broadcast and recording equipment
  • Electronic control systems

Can you restore smoke-damaged electronics?

Yes, we specialize in smoke damage electronics restoration. Our process includes complete disassembly, ultrasonic cleaning, component testing, deodorizing, and reassembly. We've successfully restored electronics after house fires, church fires, and other smoke damage scenarios.

Do you work on church and religious institution electronics?

Yes, we have extensive experience with church electronics including pipe organ systems, sound reinforcement, recording equipment, and projection systems. We understand the unique schedules and requirements of religious institutions and offer Saturday night emergency service for Sunday worship needs.

Can you restore electronics that no longer have replacement parts available?

Often yes. We maintain relationships with specialty parts suppliers and can often fabricate or adapt replacement components for vintage electronics. Our expertise includes working with obsolete technologies and finding creative solutions for discontinued equipment.

Document Preservation

What types of documents can you restore?

We preserve and restore:

  • Historic documents and manuscripts
  • Family photographs and albums
  • Books and bound volumes
  • Maps and architectural drawings
  • Certificates and diplomas
  • Business records and archives

Can you restore water-damaged documents?

Yes, water-damaged document restoration is one of our specialties. Treatment must begin immediately after damage occurs. We use freeze-drying, specialized cleaning techniques, and stabilization procedures to recover documents that might otherwise be considered total losses.

Do you provide digital preservation services?

Yes, we offer high-resolution digital scanning and archival storage solutions. Digital preservation ensures document content is preserved even if the original continues to deteriorate. We provide various digital formats suitable for different preservation and access needs.

Can you work with government and institutional archives?

Yes, we work with government agencies, museums, libraries, and other institutions throughout the DMV region. We understand archival standards and can provide specialized documentation and handling procedures required for institutional collections.
