Our Wedding Dress Restoration Process

Every wedding dress restoration begins with a detailed assessment by our certified textile conservators. We photograph the damage, test cleaning solutions on hidden areas, and create a customized restoration plan that preserves the integrity of your gown while removing all traces of damage.

Our process includes:

  1. Emergency Stabilization - Immediate damage control to prevent further harm
  2. Detailed Assessment - Professional evaluation with high-resolution documentation
  3. Custom Treatment Plan - Tailored approach based on fabric, age, and damage type
  4. Expert Restoration - Using museum-quality techniques and materials
  5. Quality Assurance - Multiple quality checks before return
  6. Preservation Packaging - Professional storage solutions for long-term preservation

Whether you need immediate consultation or emergency response, we're here to help save your wedding dress and preserve your memories.
