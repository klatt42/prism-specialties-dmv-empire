Our Story in the DMV

After a disaster hits, those impacted are changed forever. At Prism Specialties DMV, we believe in helping the people of Washington DC, Maryland, and Northern Virginia through their most trying times so we can offer reassurance and relief when they need it the most.

As an independently owned and operated franchise of Prism Specialties, we bring over 25 years of proven specialty restoration expertise directly to our local DMV community. We understand the unique challenges our region faces - from historic home restorations in Georgetown to high-tech electronics recovery in Northern Virginia's business corridor.

Whether it's residential or commercial, we deliver effective recovery solutions to help people and businesses in our community recover fast from natural disasters and other unforeseen damages.
