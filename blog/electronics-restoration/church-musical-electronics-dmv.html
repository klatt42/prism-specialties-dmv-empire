The Smoke Damage Assessment

Fire damage to electronics goes beyond what's visible on the surface:

🔥 Fire Damage Discovered

  • 💨 Smoke Infiltration: Soot coating on all circuit boards and electronic components
  • 🌡️ Heat Damage: Plastic components warped from elevated temperatures
  • 🔌 Electrical Contamination: Conductive soot causing short circuits
  • 🎛️ Control System Failure: Digital mixing console completely non-functional
  • 🔊 Speaker Damage: Smoke residue on speaker cones affecting sound quality
  • 🧪 Corrosive Effects: Acidic smoke compounds beginning to corrode metal contacts
