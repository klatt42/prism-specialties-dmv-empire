Our Wedding Dress Restoration Process

1. Comprehensive Assessment

  • Detailed photography documentation
  • Fabric composition analysis
  • Stain and damage inventory
  • Historical construction evaluation
  • Restoration feasibility report

2. Specialized Cleaning

  • pH-neutral cleaning solutions for delicate fabrics
  • Hand-cleaning for intricate beadwork and lace
  • Stain removal using conservation-grade techniques
  • Color restoration for yellowed or discolored areas
  • Gentle pressing and reshaping

3. Structural Restoration

  • Tear repair using period-appropriate techniques
  • Seam reinforcement and reconstruction
  • Bead and sequin reattachment
  • Lace patching and invisible mending
  • Bustle and closure restoration

4. Museum-Quality Preservation

  • Acid-free tissue paper and storage materials
  • Custom preservation boxes sized to your gown
  • Climate-controlled storage recommendations
  • Handling and viewing instructions
  • Long-term preservation care
