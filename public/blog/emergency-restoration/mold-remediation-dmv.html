Our Emergency Mold Remediation Process

1. Emergency Assessment (0-6 Hours)

  • Comprehensive mold inspection and air quality testing
  • Moisture source identification and mapping
  • Health risk evaluation and safety protocols
  • Containment area establishment with negative pressure
  • Emergency moisture control and dehumidification

2. Containment & Safety (6-24 Hours)

  • Full containment barriers with HEPA filtration
  • Personal protective equipment for all workers

  • Removal and disposal of contaminated materials
  • Structural cleaning and decontamination
