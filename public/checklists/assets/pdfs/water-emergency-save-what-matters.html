IMMEDIATE ACTION (0-15 MINUTES)
⚡ Electrical Safety First
Shut off electricity to affected areas if safe to do so. Stay out of standing water.
🚰 Stop the Water Source
Locate and shut off main water supply if from plumbing issue.
📞 Emergency Contacts
Call insurance company and emergency restoration services immediately.
DAMAGE PREVENTION (15 MIN - 1 HOUR)
📸 Document Everything
Take photos and videos of all damage before touching anything.
🏃‍♂️ Remove Standing Water
Use pumps, wet vacuums, or mops. First 24 hours are critical.
📚 Rescue Priority Items
Move valuable documents, electronics, and irreplaceable items to dry areas.
RECOVERY PROCESS (1-24 HOURS)
🌬️ Increase Air Circulation
Open windows, use fans and dehumidifiers to speed drying.
🧽 Remove Wet Materials
Remove carpeting and damaged materials that can't dry within 48 hours.
🧪 Monitor for Mold
Watch for mold signs. Mold can begin growing within 24-48 hours.
📝 Create Detailed Inventory
List all damaged items with photos, descriptions, and estimated values for insurance claim.
🏠 Check Hidden Areas
Inspect walls, subflooring, and HVAC systems for hidden moisture damage.

Professional Insight: Water damage restoration is most effective when started within the first 24 hours. Our certified technicians use industrial-grade equipment to extract moisture that standard fans and dehumidifiers cannot reach. Early professional intervention can reduce restoration costs by 60-80%.
